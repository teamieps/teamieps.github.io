ABOUT US

We are Eric, Demi, Sebastian, and Kyle, a team of Princeton University students who are passionate about increasing access to special education resources. As a part of the Tiger Challenge program, we have used Design Thinking with the objective of identifying gaps in the way special education services are delivered, leading us to create this site.
