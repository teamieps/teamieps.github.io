ABOUT US

SpecialEducationResources.org is a free and not-for-profit tool created by a group of students at Princeton University. This website is designed to help families connect with special education resources near them.

This tool was built by Demi Zhang, Eric Guerci, Kyle Barnes, and Sebastian Hallum Clarke through Princeton University's Tiger Challenge design thinking social innovation program. The team was advised by Rafe Steinhauer and Rob Van Varick of the Keller Center.
